2025 Hellkat 32

The Hellkats 32 is a widebody catamaran built for maximum performance. The wide 62" tunnel is dimensioned for APBA Super Stock Offshore race class and when paired with the optional Closed Cockpit Layout it's ready to go. For those seeking a truly unique aesthetic, the 32 can be done in raw clear coat carbon with optional colored carbon designs, including camo weave, herringbone weave, and honeycomb weave, offering an exotic look.

Why Choose the Hellkat 32?

The 2025 Hellkat 32 is engineered for the hardcore racer—carbon-fiber finishes, a 62″ widebody tunnel, and Mercury Racing power deliver lap‑winning speed. Whether you’re chasing APBA Super Stock records or carving wake on the lake, the Hellkat 32 blends showroom style with racehorse performance.

Specifications

  • Length: 32 ft
  • Beam: 10.5 ft
  • Engines: Twin 300R Mercury Racing
  • Fuel Capacity: 60 gallons x2
