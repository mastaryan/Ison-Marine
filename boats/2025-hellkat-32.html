2025 Hellkat 32

The Hellkats 32 is a widebody catamaran built for maximum performance. The wide 62" tunnel is dimensioned for APBA Super Stock Offshore race class and when paired with the optional Closed Cockpit Layout its ready to go. For those seeking a truly unique aesthetic, the 32 can be done in raw clear coat carbon with optional colored carbon designs, including camo weave, herringbone weave, and honeycomb weave, offering an exotic look.

Specifications

  • Length: 32 ft
  • Beam: 10.5 ft
  • Engines: Twin 300R Mercury Racing (Optional 400R or 500R Mercury Racing)
  • Fuel Capacity: 60 gallons x2 (Optional 120 gallons x2)
